Tigerplum is my individually run design studio.

I specialise in crafting meaningful brands and thoughtful web design solutions for small to medium sized businesses, start-ups, individuals and fellow creative agencies. Whilst I’m flying solo here at Tigerplum, I work within a strong network of other talented freelance designers, programmers and creative specialists – which helps keep the studio small and nimble.

I love working alongside clients who really believe in what they do, and who are looking for innovative, creative ways to speak to their audience. For me, great design tells a story – and that’s because every brand has its own story to tell.

Outside of work I’m lead creative in my two most important projects to date: my fabulous boys (with a little help from my husband of course). And living in North Devon means we spend lots of time at the beach and mucking about in the countryside – and feeling very lucky to live in such a beautiful place. Want to know more?
